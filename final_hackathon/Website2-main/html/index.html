Who we are


The kkr team consists of 3 members, Kayven, Rui Zhuo and Kien Yu. We are a group of year 1 students in computing whose goal is to use technology and artificial intelligence to shape and improve the world and the lives all around us.


What is our product?


Our theme for this hackathon is to promote awareness and concern for the general welfare of our environment. Hence, our product, EZrecycle, is a computer vision based product to detect Aluminium,Carton,Glass,Organic waste,Textiles,Woods,plastic and paper/cardboard items to be recycled and reduce the amount of litter scattered all around the world.
